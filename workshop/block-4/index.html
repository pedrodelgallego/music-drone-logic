Block 4 — Transformation

How sound is altered

Resonance, saturation, granular reconstruction, slicing, and tape degradation — taught as level into transformation: what enters the processor matters as much as the processor.

Year 2 · Domain C · Labs 013–017 · Programme L4 · Capstone C3

Engineering focus

Every lab asks: what level enters and leaves? Linear or nonlinear? Does processor order matter? Is the degradation reversible, cumulative, or generational — after one pass, three, ten?

Listening programme L4 — material under stress

  • Samples: trace one sample-based work to its sources — what survived the chain?
  • Video: tape-music interviews — where does the history of the path become audible?
