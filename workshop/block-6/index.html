Block 6 — Decision Systems

What generates the choices

Generative rules, self-playing patches, chance operations, and tuning systems — designing behavior, then curating its output. Not “random things happened.”

Year 2 · Domain E · Labs 023–026 · Programme L6 · Year capstone C5

Engineering focus

Audio path and control path, drawn as two separate diagrams. Every lab declares state (what does the system remember?), seed (can the result be reproduced?), and boundary (what may the system decide — what is forbidden?). Self-running labs pass a 10-minute unattended test.

Listening programme L6 — systems & chance

  • Records: Cage chance-operations works · Eno Music for Airports · Lucier I Am Sitting in a Room
  • Samples: deconstruction — what was decided by rule vs by hand in one generative work
  • Video: Eno generative-music talks; the Lucier film — the room as the system
  • Deliverable: system analyses + one stolen principle, evaluated at C5 forthcoming
