04 — Sound palette

Sound palette

A small set of elements. Each one earns its place by what it does, not by what it adds.

Bass sequence

  • Short, repetitive, slightly acidic, rhythmic
  • 3–5 notes, filter-driven, minor or dark intervals
  • Strong enough to loop for minutes without fatigue
  • Wavetable / Harmo / SuperWave with low-pass filter

Drone

  • Long, evolving, slightly unstable, spacious
  • One sustained note with slow LFO modulation
  • Textural rather than melodic
  • Processed with Hybrid Reverb and Echo

Atmosphere

  • Resampled MicroFreak drone
  • Reversed audio, frozen reverb tails
  • Filtered noise or stretched textures
  • Felt more than clearly heard

Drums

  • Four-on-the-floor kick, offbeat closed hat
  • One simple repeating percussion element
  • No fills, no crashes, no over-programming
  • Minimal by design

Effects — Ableton stock only

Stock effects are sufficient and they force focus.
